Tabata Challenges

You may complete a Tabata Challenge once per day. It is meant to be a good way to top off your exercise for the day since it only takes 4 minutes. See the list of approved workouts and more information below.

  1. Burpees
  2. Jump Squats & Switch Jump Lunges
  3. Push Ups & Tuck Jumps
  4. Supermans & Push Ups
  5. Mountain Climbers & Jump Squats
  6. Robot Push Ups & V-Ups

Try to vary which Tabata Challenge you choose each day, since doing the same Challenge 7 days in a row will destroy one muscle group. I encourage you to submit Challenges yourself if you would like to see them and I will incorporate them in future games.

Tabata Challenges consist of 8 rounds of 20 seconds on and 10 seconds recovery. If there is one movement, you do that movement 8 times. If there are two movements, you do each 4 times. It should always sum up to being a 4 minute workout.
