Daily Bonuses

This scoring category is for points-per-day (PPD) activities, including Tabata Challenges, Head-to-Head match ups, and daily stretching.

Tabata Challenges: 1500 PPD

You can only complete the Tabata challenge once per day. It is meant to be a good way to top off your exercise for the day since it only takes 4 minutes.

Tabata workouts consist of 8 rounds of 20 seconds on and 10 seconds recovery. I recommend choosing a different workout from the list of approved Tabata workouts every day, since you don't want to overwork one muscle group. I encourage you to submit challenges yourself if you would like to see them and I will incorporate them in future games.

This is probably a new concept for many of you. I highly encourage you to google the Tabata methodology for yourselves to learn the procedure, theory, and benefits. It is a form of HIIT (high intensity interval training) that helps with endurance and recovery (very useful in ultimate since we do lots of sprinting). It is also touted as a phenomenal way to burn fat due to EPOC (excess post-exercise oxygen consumption). EPOC basically means your metabolism shoots through the roof because your muscles are still trying to gather oxygen hours after you have finished exercising.

Stretching: 1000 PPD

You can only earn points for this once per day (thought additional stretching is encouraged). Try to do this at least once a day after your workout. You must stretch for at least 10 minutes to earn this bonus.

If yoga is part of your workout that day, then you can count this bonus for the day without spending an extra 10 minutes stretching.
